Important context

This is an independent educational resource. It is not a Government of Canada website. Entries are based on public or primary sources where available. Where a direct source is not available, the entry names the report, law or record to verify.

Source standard and update method

Use primary sources first: legislation, court or tribunal decisions, government reports, audits, parliamentary records and official public service publications. For every new entry, name the event, the source, what changed and what remained unresolved. Avoid unsourced claims, promotional wording and personal allegations.
