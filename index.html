From Exclusion to Accountability

A history of anti-racism, equity, inclusion and accessibility in the Public Service of Canada. Each entry records what changed and what remained outstanding, with a source you can check.

How to read this record: each entry notes, from the cited sources, what changed after the measure and what remained outstanding. Statuses are stated as of July 2026 and carry no editorial characterization.

This is an independent educational resource, not a Government of Canada site. Links were last checked in July 2026. Reports that predate the web are cited by title and year; most can be found through publications.gc.ca or the Library of Parliament. If you spot an error, verify against the linked primary source and report it to the site maintainer.
